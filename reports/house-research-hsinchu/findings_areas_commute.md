## Housing Criteria Match by Area

Target criteria:
- 屋齡 < 10 years (built after ~2016)
- 3+ bedrooms
- Under 25M NTD
- Prefer 獨棟 (standalone house) or 頂樓 (top floor of elevator building)

### 1. 關埔重劃區 (Guangpu Planned Area) — ⭐ Top Pick for Elevator Buildings
- **Building types**: Mostly high-rise elevators (10–24F). Few 獨棟.
- **3BR price (≤10yr)**: **22–35M NTD** → tight on 25M budget; may need <45坪 unit.
- **頂樓 availability**: Yes, but premium price (~28–35M).
- **獨棟**: Extremely rare. Almost no 透天 in 關埔.
- **Verdict**: Best commute. Elevator 3BR feasible at ~22–28M, but 頂樓 likely over budget.

### 2. 新竹東區 (Hsinchu East District, outside 關埔) — ⭐ Good Balance
- **Building types**: Mix of elevators and older 公寓/透天.
- **3BR price (≤10yr)**: **18–28M NTD** for elevators; **20–35M** for newer 透天.
- **頂樓 (elevator building)**: ~20–25M feasible.
- **獨棟/透天**: Very limited <10yr inventory in budget.
- **Verdict**: Good commute. Elevator 頂樓 feasible.

### 3. 竹東·二重埔 (Zhudong / Erchongpu) — ⭐ Best Value for 獨棟
- **Building types**: Many new 透天 communities (e.g., 三圓建設, 昌禾). Also elevators.
- **3BR price (≤10yr)**: **12–18M NTD** for elevators; **15–22M NTD** for 透天/獨棟.
- **頂樓 availability**: Yes, ~14–18M.
- **獨棟/透天 availability**: Good supply. Modern 社區透天 ~16–22M. **Best 獨棟 bet.**
- **Commute**: 12–20 min via 68 freeway — very predictable, no traffic lights.
- **Verdict**: **Best match overall.** Under budget for 獨棟, good commute.

### 4. 寶山鄉 (Baoshan) — ✅ Good for 獨棟 on Budget
- **Building types**: 透天/獨棟為主. Few elevators.
- **3BR 透天 (≤10yr)**: **12–18M NTD**.
- **獨棟 availability**: Good. Rural/suburban feel.
- **頂樓**: Rare (few elevators).
- **Commute**: 12–20 min via 寶山路.
- **Verdict**: Under budget, good commute, but very rural (few amenities).

### 5. 竹北 (Zhubei) — ⚠️ Expensive & Traffic
- **Building types**: Mix of elevators and 透天.
- **3BR price (≤10yr)**: **25–45M NTD** for elevators; **30–50M+** for 透天.
- **頂樓**: 28–40M — mostly over budget.
- **獨棟**: Mostly over 30M.
- **Commute**: 20–35 min. Risky during 8-9AM peak.
- **Verdict**: Mostly over budget for <10yr 3BR. Traffic is a wildcard.

### 6. 香山 (Xiangshan) — ✅ Budget Option
- **Building types**: 透天為主.
- **3BR 透天 (≤10yr)**: **12–20M NTD**.
- **獨棟 availability**: Decent supply.
- **頂樓**: Rare.
- **Commute**: 20–30 min via 茄苳景觀大道.
- **Verdict**: Good budget option for 獨棟. Commute is ~25 min edge case.

### 7. 穹林 (Qionglin) — ✅ Budget, Longer Commute
- **Building types**: 透天 communities.
- **3BR 透天 (≤10yr)**: **10–16M NTD**.
- **獨棟 availability**: Good, new 透天 communities.
- **Commute**: 20–30 min via 68 freeway.
- **Verdict**: Cheap for 獨棟, but commute is on the edge of 30 min.

## Rankings by Criteria Fit

### For 獨棟 (Standalone House) Preference
1. **竹東·二重埔** — Best balance of price (15–22M), commute (12–20 min), and 獨棟 supply.
2. **穹林** — Cheapest 獨棟 (10–16M), but commute edge case (25–30 min).
3. **寶山** — Rural 透天 at 12–18M, peaceful but minimal amenities.
4. **香山** — 12–20M, commute ~25 min.

### For 頂樓 (Top Floor Elevator) Preference
1. **關埔重劃區** — Best area, but 頂樓 may push 28–35M (over budget).
2. **新竹東區** — 頂樓 20–25M, best value for 頂樓 under 25M.
3. **竹東·二重埔** — 頂樓 14–18M, cheapest option.
4. **竹北** — 頂樓 28–40M, mostly over budget.

## Data Sources & Caveats
- Price ranges are approximate based on recent public market data from 實價登錄 and real estate platforms (591, 樂居, 信義房屋).
- Commute times are estimates for **8:00–9:00 AM weekday peak** using primary routes.
- 竹北 commute is highly variable (±15 min depending on weather/accidents at 經國橋).
- 68快速道路 (Toufen–Hsinchu Freeway) has no traffic lights and is the most predictable route.
- Prices for <10yr 3BR/獨棟 under 25M are most realistic in 竹東·二重埔, 寶山, 香山, and 穹林.
