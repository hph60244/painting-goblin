## Trap System Mechanics (Inferred)

Based on the Deception series tradition and what is known about Trap Girls:

1. **Three Placement Categories**: Floor, Wall, Ceiling - consistent with the series
2. **Combination/Chain System**: The series is famous for combo chains where traps are activated in sequence. Trap Girls likely had a similar or simplified version
3. **Charge Times**: Each trap has a cooldown/recharge period
4. **Damage Types**:
   - Direct damage traps (Mega Rock, Needle Floor, Arrow Shooter)
   - Movement/position traps (Attack Wall, Spring Floor, Smash Floor)
   - Status/restraint traps (Bear Trap, Spider Net)
   - Comedic traps (Washtub, Toy Hammer, Snowball)
5. **Costume Break**: As damage accumulates on Medium characters, their costumes break, likely tied to HP thresholds

## Comparison with Main Series Trap System

The main Deception series (影牢 〜刻命館 真章〜) features:
- **3 traps per category** (floor/wall/ceiling) per mission, 9 total
- **Combo system**: Up to 10-hit combos by chaining traps within a time window
- **Trap development tree**: Spend Ark points to unlock stronger traps
- **Ark system**: Points earned from trap hits, combos, and maximum damage

Trap Girls likely simplified this system for the browser/social game format while retaining the core trap-luring gameplay loop.
