## Game Overview

"影牢 トラップガールズ" (Deception Trap Girls) was a browser-based social/online game operated by DMM Games. It was a spin-off of Koei Tecmo's Deception (影牢/刻命館) series, which is known for its "trap action" gameplay where the player lures enemies into deadly traps rather than engaging in direct combat.

### Key Differences from Main Series

The game content differs significantly from traditional Deception series titles:

1. **Personified Traps (擬人化)**: Instead of placing abstract traps, the traps are personified as female characters called "メディウム" (Mediums). Each trap type is represented by a unique character.

2. **Costume Break System**: When damage accumulates, the Medium characters' clothing breaks/cracks (衣装ブレイク), a characteristic feature not present in the main series.

3. **Browser/Social Game Format**: Unlike the single-player console games, this was an online browser game, likely featuring gacha mechanics for collecting trap characters.

4. **Diverse Character Themes**: The personified trap characters have various themed appearances including:
   - Fantasy types (pirates, hunters)
   - Modern types (idols, bowlers, military personnel)
   - Traditional Japanese (yukata)
   - Occupational (chefs)

5. **Core Mechanic Preserved**: The fundamental gameplay of setting traps and luring villagers/enemies into them remains consistent with the traditional Deception series.

## Confirmed Terminology

- **メディウム (Medium)**: The term used for personified trap characters
- **トラップ (Trap)**: Individual trap abilities corresponding to classic Deception series traps
- **衣装ブレイク (Costume Break)**: Clothing damage mechanic
