### Known General Information About the Akudaikan Series (for context)

The Akudaikan series is developed by **Global A Entertainment** and published for PS2. The games are strategy/simulation titles where the player plays as a corrupt feudal magistrate (悪代官). The gameplay involves:

- Defending your manor/territory from invading heroes (justice-seeking characters)
- Setting traps throughout your residence
- Managing resources and hiring retainers
