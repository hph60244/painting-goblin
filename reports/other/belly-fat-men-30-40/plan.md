# Research Plan: 30~40歲男性瘦肚子的方法

## Main Research Question
What are the most effective methods for men aged 30-40 to lose belly fat?

## Subtopics

### 1. 飲食與營養策略 (Diet & Nutrition)
Investigate dietary approaches specifically effective for reducing visceral fat in men 30-40: calorie deficit strategies, macronutrient composition (protein, fiber), intermittent fasting, eating patterns, and foods that target belly fat.

### 2. 運動與訓練方法 (Exercise & Training)
Research exercise protocols for belly fat reduction: HIIT vs. steady-state cardio, resistance training impact on metabolic rate, core exercises, optimal training frequency, and the role of NEAT (non-exercise activity thermogenesis).

### 3. 生活型態與荷爾蒙因素 (Lifestyle & Hormonal Factors)
Explore how sleep quality, stress management (cortisol), testosterone levels, alcohol consumption, and daily habits affect belly fat accumulation and loss in men aged 30-40.

## Synthesis Approach
Combine findings from all three subtopics to provide a comprehensive, actionable guide with specific recommendations, citing sources and their credibility.
