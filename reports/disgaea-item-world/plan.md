# Research Plan: Disgaea Item World (道具界)

## Main Research Question
Analyze the rules and gameplay of the "Item World (道具界)" from the Disgaea series.

## Subtopics

### 1. Basic Rules and Structure
- How Item World is entered and navigated
- Floor generation mechanics (randomly generated dungeons)
- Leveling up items through clearing floors
- Exit/Gate mechanics

### 2. Core Mechanics
- Geo Panels and Geo Symbols
- Specialists (住人) - capturing, moving, combining
- Bonus Gauge system
- Pirates and invasions
- Item General/King/God battles

### 3. Series Evolution
- Changes across Disgaea 1 through 7
- Notable additions and removals in each iteration
- Key differences in Item World between games
