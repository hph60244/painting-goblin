# Findings: Core Mechanics of Item World

## Geo Panels and Geo Symbols
- Color-coded panels (red, green, blue, yellow, purple, cyan) appear on floors
- Geo Symbols placed on colored panels grant bonuses or penalties to all units on same-colored panels
- Effects can stack if multiple Geo Symbols are on the same panel
- Destroying a Geo Symbol causes chain reactions, damaging units on affected panels and changing panel colors
- Geo Symbols can be lifted and thrown like characters

## Specialists (住人 / Innocents)
- Innocents are creatures living inside items that grant stat bonuses
- Types include: Gladiator (ATK), Sentry (DEF), Tutor (INT), Physician (RES), Marksman (HIT), Coach (SPD), Dietician (HP), Master (SP)
- Innocents start "unsubdued" (red icon); must be subdued (yellow icon) to be transferable
- Subdued innocents can be moved between items freely
- Unique innocents: Teacher (levels up other innocents), Mediator (merges innocents into dual-stats), Enforcer (transfers subdued innocents), Collector (increases item rarity)
- Statistician innocent (increases EXP) is only acquired through Reverse Pirating

## Bonus Gauge
- Completing combat objectives (combos, special attacks, etc.) fills a bonus gauge
- Higher bonus scores yield better bonus items at mission completion
- Bonus gauge carries over to Item World floors

## Pirates and Invasions
- When ending a turn, there is a chance pirates will invade the floor
- Pirates can appear in waves (first wave end of turn 1-2, second wave possible next turn)
- Defeating pirates unlocks the "Create a Pirate Crew" bill
- Having a pirate crew grants access to Reverse Pirating

## Reverse Pirating
- Available every 20 floors in a completed item
- Players set sail with a pirate ship to revisit cleared floors
- Level Spheres in Reverse Pirating are always Yellow (+10) or Red (+15)
- Essential for reaching the last 50-75 item levels
- Level Spheres must be on the ship before it takes off to count

## Item General / King / God Battles
- Every 10 floors, a boss appears at the center of the map
- Boss types and stat multipliers (Disgaea 4):
  - Floor 10: Item General (+0.1 multiplier)
  - Floor 20: Item General (+0.1)
  - Floor 30: Item King (+0.2)
  - Floor 40: Item General (+0.1)
  - Floor 50: Item General (+0.1)
  - Floor 60: Item King (+0.2)
  - Floor 70: Item General (+0.1)
  - Floor 80: Item General (+0.1)
  - Floor 90: Item King (+0.2)
  - Floor 100: Item God (+0.3)
- "Double-killing" bosses: kill boss → Mr. Gency → re-enter → kill again for double multiplier
- Item Generals/Kings/Gods always carry powerful equipment worth stealing

## Mystery Gates / Rooms
- Green-colored gates with "?" marks that lead to random rooms
- Types include:
  - Ambush Room: 7 giant monsters; defeat within 2 turns (+5 levels) or stall (+2 levels)
  - Fortune Teller Room: random fortune (Super Great Luck +5, Good +3, Bad -3, Terrible -5)
  - Merchant rooms with rare items
  - Combat encounters with unique rewards
- Mystery Gates reset the floor layout when entered

## Level Spheres
- Colored spheres that grant bonus item levels when lifted and carried through floor completion
  - Green: +5 levels
  - Yellow: +10 levels
  - Red: +15 levels
- Spawn naturally, from Item Growth Route, or from Event Areas

## Event Areas
- Purple columns requiring a certain number of units to stand within them
- When requirement is met: spawns treasure chest, Mystery Gate, or Level Sphere

## Item World Senate (Disgaea 4+)
- Every 10 floors, chance to enter Innocent Town
- Mushroom NPC allows passing bills for the item
- Bills like "A Little More [STAT] Growth" and "More [STAT] Growth"
- Bill passing further increases stat multipliers based on bosses killed
- Maximum of 6 bills can be passed

## Item Path vs Innocent Boost Path (Disgaea 4)
- Split paths every 5 floors (except last floor)
- Item Growth Path: bonus levels (+1 to +3 per entry), guarantees ~+30 levels
- Innocent Boost Path: improves innocent subduing bonuses, can spawn new innocents
