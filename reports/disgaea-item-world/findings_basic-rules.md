# Findings: Basic Rules and Structure of Item World

## Overview
The Item World (道具界) is a core gameplay mechanic in the Disgaea series. Every item in the game (weapons, armor, consumables, etc.) contains a randomly generated dungeon called the "Item World." By entering and progressing through this dungeon, players can increase the item's stats and unlock its full potential.

## Entering and Navigating the Item World
- Players access the Item World from the central hub via the "Item World" gateway
- All games have an Item World tutorial during Chapter 3
- Progress is measured in "floors" — each floor is a procedurally generated grid-based battlefield
- To clear a floor, players can either defeat all enemies OR reach the exit portal (red gate)
- Items gain +1 level per floor cleared (either by killing enemies or skipping to the portal)

## Floor Count Based on Rarity
- Normal (Common) items: 30 floors maximum
- Rare items: 60 floors maximum
- Legendary items: 100 floors maximum
- In Disgaea 6: infinite floors (procedurally generated), with key enemies every 10 floors and Item Gods every 100 floors

## Item Leveling
- Items start at level 0 and can reach a maximum level (varies by game)
- Disgaea 1-5: maximum item level is 300
- Disgaea D2: maximum item level is 300 (or 999 in Rasetsu mode)
- Disgaea 6+: higher level caps introduced
- When 100 floors are completed, floors reset to 1 but stats continue to increase

## Exit Mechanics
- Mr. Gency's Exit: a key item that allows players to leave the Item World at any time, preserving progress
- Without a Mr. Gency's Exit, players must reach a milestone (every 10 floors) or die to exit
- StrategyWiki emphasizes: "always make sure there is a Mr. Gency's Exit in your Item Bag"
