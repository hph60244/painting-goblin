## 2. Layout Size Analysis (Pros & Cons)

### 2-Bedroom: 31.83 坪 (~105 m²)

| Pros | Cons |
|---|---|
| Spacious for a 2BR — rare in Taiwan pre-sale market where 2BR often runs 22-28坪 | At 31+ 坪, total price may approach entry-level 3BR of other builders, hurting resale |
| True 2BR with potential for a study/den (typical 圓湖 quality finishes) | Higher management fee floor for a 2-person household: 31.83 × 80 = **2,546 元/月** |
| Dual-aspect (corner unit) provides cross-ventilation, reducing AC costs | 31 坪 in a 31~34% public facility ratio means ~20-21 坪 indoor + balcony — generous but less efficient than older buildings |
| Attracts DINK (dual-income-no-kids) 竹科 couples who want space | May be harder to flip vs 3BR since target buyer pool for 2BR at this price point is narrower |

### 3-Bedroom: 42.34 坪 (~140 m²)

| Pros | Cons |
|---|---|
| **Sweet spot** — most liquid layout for 芎林 pre-sale; primary target for 竹科家庭 | Management fee: 42.34 × 80 = **3,387 元/月** |
| Indoor usable ~27-29 坪 = comfortable 3BR with 2 baths + standard kitchen | Price at 60+ 萬/坪 → ~2,540 萬+ total; tight for young engineers |
| Dual-aspect corner = true 3-side采光, rare in this segment | 42 坪 range competes directly with 竹科主人9 (finished/completed units) which may sell at discount |
| Standard 圓湖 floor plan = no wasted走廊; high efficiency | — |

### 4-Bedroom: 53.09 坪 (~175 m²)

| Pros | Cons |
|---|---|
| **Largest layout** — essentially an entry-level luxury product for the area | Management fee: 53.09 × 80 = **4,247 元/月** — significant monthly carry |
| Dual-aspect corner + end-unit positioning maximizes natural light | At 60 萬/坪 → ~3,185 萬; at 65 萬/坪 → ~3,450 萬. This price range faces competition from竹北二手屋 (mature area amenities) |
| 53 坪 = true 4BR + maids room or storage; suits multi-generational living | 芎林 vs 竹北 premium: buyers paying ~60+ 萬/坪 in 芎林 may hesitate vs 竹北 2nd-hand options in the same price band |
| Lowest supply → scarcity premium for resale | Long wait time (2028) for large outlay — opportunity cost of capital tied up for 3+ years |

## 3. Financial Implications

### 3.1 Pre-Sale Delivery Timeline Risk (Completion: August 2028)

- **3+ year wait** from mid-2026 (if purchasing now).
- **Typical payment schedule for Taiwan pre-sale:**
  - 10-15% down (signing + engineering)
  - 10-15% during construction (staged payments over 2-3 years)
  - 70-80% at completion (mortgage)
- **Opportunity cost:** Funds tied to construction payments could alternatively earn ~4-5% in diversified investments over 3 years.
- **Market risk:** 2028 delivery means betting on 新竹 housing market conditions 3+ years out.
  - Semiconductor cycle dependency: if TSMC/竹科 slows, 芎林 (as a commuter bedroom community) is more exposed than 竹北 core.
  - However, 圓湖 is a known brand with 10 phases of execution — default/completion risk is low relative to unknown builders.

### 3.2 Management Fee Analysis

**Formula:** 坪數 × 80 元/坪/月

| Layout | Monthly Fee | Annual Fee | 5-Year Total |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2BR (31.83 坪) | 2,546 元 | 30,557 元 | 152,784 元 |
| 3BR (42.34 坪) | 3,387 元 | 40,646 元 | 203,232 元 |
| 4BR (53.09 坪) | 4,247 元 | 50,966 元 | 254,832 元 |

**Comments:**
- 80 元/坪/月 is **above average** for 芎林/竹東 (typical range: 50-70 元). Justified by:
  - 31-34% public facility ratio (higher amenities)
  - EV pre-wiring infrastructure
  - 千坪基地 maintenance costs (landscaping, security)
- Monthly fee adds to carrying cost — relevant for investors calculating rental yield.

### 3.3 Parking Cost Implications

- **Ratio 1:1.39** (110 spaces for 79 units) means each unit can buy at least 1 space; some can buy 2.
- Typical 芎林 pre-sale parking price range: **120~160 萬 per平面車位** (estimated, not confirmed).
- For a 3BR buyer: unit ~2,540 萬 + parking ~140 萬 = total ~2,680 萬.
- EV pre-wiring adds future-proofing value vs older communities (no retrofit costs).

## 6. Summary Assessment

**Strengths:**
- 圓湖 has strong brand equity in 芎林 (10 phases, all sold out)
- Dual-aspect corner units for all layouts is a legit differentiator (very rare)
- 文德特區 is the most mature area in 芎林 with full amenities
- 1:1.39 parking ratio is generous; EV pre-wired is future-proof
- RC structure + quality materials (TOTO, 林內, 力霸鋁窗 per brand standards)

**Weaknesses/Risks:**
- 60-65 萬/坪 for 芎林 is aggressive — buyer needs to believe 芎林 will appreciate further
- 3+ year completion wait (Aug 2028) creates macro risk (semiconductor cycle, interest rates)
- Management fee at 80元/坪/月 is above local average
- 4BR at ~3,200萬+ faces competition from 竹北 resale (more central)
- Only 2 buildings with 79 units — small community, higher per-unit maintenance burden

**Best layout pick:** 3BR (42.34 坪) — most liquid, best target-buyer fit for 竹科 families, and the dual-aspect corner benefit is maximized at this size point.
