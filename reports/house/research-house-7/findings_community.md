# Community Research: 富宇悅讀四季 (Fuyu Reading Four Seasons)

**Location:** 慈祥路1號, 3號, 東區, 新竹市
**Area:** 關埔重劃區 (Guanpu Replanned Area), near Costco Hsinchu
**Developer:** 富宇建設 (Fuyu Construction)
**Building Type:** High-rise apartment (RC, 地上24層/地下5層)
**Age:** Built ~2015-2017 (approx. 8-10 years old as of 2025)
**Unit Sizes:** ~42-71 ping (including parking)
**Parking:** 坡道平面車位 (ramp-style flat parking)
**Price Trend:**
- 2022 presale: ~80萬/坪
- 2025 current: ~70-73萬/坪 (latest transaction 2025/04: ~57.7萬/坪 for 3BR)

---

## 1. Developer Reputation: 富宇建設

富宇建設 has a **mixed to negative reputation** among Taiwanese homebuyers on PTT forums:

- **"三線建商" (third-tier builder):** A PTT user described 富宇 as a "third-line builder" in Taichung who "comes to Hsinchu and acts like they're special, opening each project at record-high prices" — suggesting they are overpriced relative to their quality tier.
  - *Source: PTT home-sale, musicaledio, 2024/11/17*
  - URL: https://www.ptt.cc/bbs/home-sale/M.1731840005.A.BA4.html

- **"富宇不推" (doesn't recommend):** Multiple PTT commenters explicitly state they do not recommend Fuyu.
  - *Source: PTT home-sale, a50847, 2025/02/19*
  - URL: https://www.ptt.cc/bbs/home-sale/M.1739849494.A.3E1.html

- **"別去踩雷" (don't go step on a landmine):** Another user warned that Fuyu's quality is inferior compared to other builders.
  - *Source: PTT home-sale, chanel1259, 2025/02/18*
  - URL: https://www.ptt.cc/bbs/home-sale/M.1739849494.A.3E1.html

- **Slightly better for urban projects:** One user noted 富宇's urban projects are "okay" but left the comment about coastal projects unfinished ("海線的就..."), implying coastal/outskirts projects are worse.
  - *Source: PTT home-sale, Aligoo0815, 2025/02/19*
  - URL: https://www.ptt.cc/bbs/home-sale/M.1739849494.A.3E1.html

- **Some positive feedback:** A buyer who ultimately purchased a Fuyu unit in 沙鹿 said their project was "還行" (acceptable), noting only that rival projects also had issues.
  - *Source: PTT home-sale, weifin, 2025/02/21*
  - URL: https://www.ptt.cc/bbs/home-sale/M.1739849494.A.3E1.html

## 2. Known Quality Issues

**Water leakage in sibling communities:** A critical finding — a PTT user reported that two other 富宇 projects in the same 新竹關埔 area (權峰 and 富春居) have **water leakage problems**.

> "權峰和富春居都有漏水…"
> (Both 權峰 and 富春居 have water leakage...)

This is directly relevant since 富宇悅讀四季 is built by the same developer in the same area, suggesting potential systemic quality issues.
- *Source: PTT home-sale, raya61305, 2022/10/21*
- URL: https://www.ptt.cc/bbs/home-sale/M.1666270962.A.C2B.html

## 3. Community Management, Cleanliness, Security, Noise

**No specific reviews found** regarding the management, cleanliness, or security of this specific community. The available data focuses on pricing and developer quality.

**Noise considerations:**
- 富宇悅讀四季 is located in the 關埔 area, directly adjacent to Costco Hsinchu on 慈雲路 / 埔頂一路 — a high-traffic commercial zone.
- The construction of the building itself (24 floors above ground, 5 below) was completed ~2020, so construction-related noise should no longer be an issue.
- The area around 慈雲路 / 好市多 is a busy commercial corridor, which could contribute to ambient traffic noise.

## 4. Resident Feedback

No direct resident testimonials were found for this specific community. Most discussion focuses on the developer's track record and pricing.
