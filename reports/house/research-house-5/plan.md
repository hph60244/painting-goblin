# House Search Research Plan: Hsinchu / Zhudong / Qionglin

## Main Question
Find houses (3+ bedrooms, 15-25 million NTD) for sale in Hsinchu East District, Zhudong, and Qionglin areas, excluding accident properties and nuisance facilities. Detached house preferred (within 10 years); elevator building in prime area acceptable (within 15 years).

## Subtopic 1: East District (Hsinchu) Listings
Search for properties in Hsinchu East District meeting the criteria. Focus on 591.com.tw and other Taiwan real estate platforms.

## Subtopic 2: Zhudong Township Listings
Search for properties in Zhudong (竹東) meeting the criteria. Focus on standalone houses (透天) under 10 years and elevator buildings under 15 years.

## Subtopic 3: Qionglin Township Listings
Search for properties in Qionglin (芎林) meeting the criteria. Focus on standalone houses (透天) under 10 years.

## Synthesis
Combine findings from all three areas into a comparative report with recommendations.
