# Hololive Fan-Made Voice Clip & Sound Effect Download Repositories

## Research Findings — May 24, 2026

---

## 1. Nyaa.si (Torrent Index — Largest Voice Pack Archive)

- **URL:** https://nyaa.si/?f=0&c=2_0&q=hololive+voice
- **Type:** BitTorrent index (Fan-uploaded official voice packs)
- **Audio Types:**
  - Official Hololive voice packs (seasonal, birthday, anniversary, ASMR, system voices)
  - Character song audio rips
  - Voice packs for all branches: Hololive JP, EN, ID
- **Formats/Quality:**
  - FLAC (lossless, sometimes 48kHz/24bit hi-res)
  - MP3 (lossy, various bitrates)
  - Sizes range from a few MiB (single talent packs) to 76+ GiB (mega-collections)
- **Notes:**
  - Largest publicly indexed collection of Hololive voice packs on the internet
  - Fan-uploaded; these are re-distributions of official paid voice packs from Hololive's BOOTH stores
  - Requires a BitTorrent client to download
  - Some torrents are very old (2020) and may have low seed counts
  - No account needed; anonymous downloading supported

## 2. Ragtag Archive (Video/Stream Archive)

- **URL:** https://archive.ragtag.moe/about
- **Type:** Fan-run video archive (open source)
- **Audio Types:**
  - Full Hololive stream recordings (highest quality available on YouTube)
  - Chat logs, descriptions, captions backed up alongside video
- **Formats/Quality:**
  - Original YouTube quality (up to 1080p/4K video, AAC/Opus audio)
  - Backs up everything available on the YouTube video page
- **Notes:**
  - Only backs up publicly available content (no members-only or unarchived streams)
  - Project started Feb 20, 2021; content deleted before that date is not available
  - Open source project — can fork and run your own instance
  - Discord server available for questions/requests
  - Contact: @kitsune_cw on Twitter or Discord

## Key Takeaways

1. **Nyaa.si is the primary source** for downloading complete official voice packs (torrent-based, up to 76+ GiB collections, FLAC/MP3).
2. **Ragtag Archive** is the best source for full stream recordings (public videos only, highest YouTube quality).
3. **Soundboard sites** (101soundboards, Voicy, Myinstants) are best for short meme clips/SFX, not full voice packs.
4. **Piapro** offers official instrumental tracks for free (Hololive's own account).
5. **No single fan site** comprehensively archives all Hololive audio; the ecosystem is fragmented across torrents, soundboards, and video archives.
