# Research Plan: Hololive Fan-made Voice/SE Download Sites

## Main Research Question
尋找Hololive的粉絲剪輯語音SE下載點，例如 voice button 類型的網站 (Find Hololive fan-made voice clip/SE download sites, e.g., voice button type sites)

## Subtopics

### Subtopic 1: Hololive Voice Button Websites
Investigate dedicated voice button sites for Hololive members (e.g., voicebutton.net, hololive-themed soundboards). Find site URLs, which members are covered, and how they work.

### Subtopic 2: Fan-made Voice Clip & SE Download Repositories
Find fan-run repositories, archives, and download sites for Hololive voice clips, sound effects (SE), and audio resources. Look for sites like Hololive fan wikis, audio dump sites, Discord archives.

### Subtopic 3: Hololive Sound Effect Collections & Tools
Search for downloadable Hololive sound effect collections, voice banks, and community tools for extracting/collecting Hololive audio. Include resources like Hololive OTTO (voice synthesizer tools), and SE collections on sites like Pixiv, Booth, or GitHub.

## Synthesis
Combine findings from all subtopics into a comprehensive list of resources with URLs, descriptions, and notes on how to access/download Hololive fan voice clips and SE.
