game writing:
Plot,
Character,
and Lore.
Not all games need to focus

In fact, some

you knock it out of the park on all of them,
it's virtually impossible to do so

So let's define these
three pillars of game writing.
Plot is simple.
It's the actual storyline
of the piece.
It's what you get if you strip away

It's also one
of the easiest to confuse.
Take Final Fantasy games,

People usually talk about Final Fantasy

Character writing is the aspect
